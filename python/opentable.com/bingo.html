Dining Points

Earn points every time you dine

Every time you dine with OpenTable, you earn Dining Rewards Points that can be redeemed for dining cheques usable at most OpenTable restaurants.

To find out more about dining points, visit our community knowledgebase.

Fine Print: Points are awarded once OpenTable receives confirmation that diners were seated. No points are awarded for cancellations or no-shows. Please allow up to 48 hours after dining for points to appear in your account.
